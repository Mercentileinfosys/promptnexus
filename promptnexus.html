What makes a good AI prompt?

A good AI prompt is specific, contextual, and clear. The best prompts often include these four elements: 1) **Role:** Tell the AI who to be (e.g., "Act as a travel expert"). 2) **Task:** Clearly define what you want it to do (e.g., "Create a 7-day itinerary for a trip to Japan"). 3) **Context:** Provide important background information (e.g., "The trip is for a couple who loves food and history"). 4) **Format:** Specify how you want the output (e.g., "Present the information in a day-by-day table"). Our generator helps you build all these elements effortlessly.
